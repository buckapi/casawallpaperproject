STEP 3 OF 5

Mural Information

ℹ

Murals are usually not calculated by rolls.

They are ordered by the total width and height of the wall area.

If the mural covers more than one wall, add all wall widths together.

Recommended ordering allowance:

  • Add 6 inches to the total width.
  • Add 4 inches to the total height.

This allows extra material for trimming, alignment, and installation adjustments.

♡

You will enter this size when purchasing your mural.
