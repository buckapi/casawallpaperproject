MURAL INFORMATION

Mural Information

ℹ

Murals are usually not calculated by rolls.

They are ordered using the total width and height of the wall area.

If the mural covers more than one wall, add all wall widths together.

Recommended ordering allowance:

  • Add 6 inches to the total width.
  • Add 4 inches to the total height.

This extra material helps with trimming, alignment, and installation adjustments.

♡

Enter this final size when purchasing your mural.
